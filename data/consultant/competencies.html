How the framework works

The core competencies detail the required skills and behaviours needed to perform your job successfully. Professional competencies are the ‘even better if’s’ — the differential superior knowledge, skills, and abilities that elevate your impact.

  • Benchmark yourself against each competency and identify what you need to meet the expected level.
  • Use the competencies to shape your development plan and discuss it at your next PEP.
  • If you’ve mastered these, review the next role in your pathway or explore potential next steps.
Your role: Consultant

Build trusted, strategic partnerships with clients and drive long-term Measurable Business Value. Own the client relationship, understand broader business and employee experience challenges, and ensure every engagement delivers outcomes aligned to their priorities.

You act as the voice of the client internally, championing strategic alignment, commercial clarity, and project relevance. You sit at the heart of the high-performing triangle (with DoCs and PMs), where partnership, strategy, and execution combine to create transformative client experiences.
